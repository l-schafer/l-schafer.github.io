Lights Out - Navigation Bar

  • Category: Mobile Development (Android : Java)
  • Course: Mobile App Development
  • Completed: 4 April, 2022

Overview

This app serves as a conceptual extension of the "Lights Out" game.
Instead of buttons switching between multiple activities, fragments are implemented in conjunction with a bottom navigation bar, providing a more professional looking implementation of the Lights Out game.
This allows for a better looking UI implementing the bottom nav bar, but creates a more complex program.

Challenges

  • Bottom Navigation Menu Bar
  • Transitioning Activities into Fragments
