The Band Database

  • Category: Mobile Development (Android : Java)
  • Course: Mobile App Development
  • Completed: 4 April, 2022

Overview

This app was created as a continuation of our introduction to fractal containers in android.
Fractal containers are implemented for each band on the corresponding information page. The band list (main screen) is also a view recycler. Meaning that if we had 100 bands in the app, we would not actually render 100 text views. Instead, views are rendered as-needed. And information is dynamically assigned to text views depending on the scroll direction.
This app also introduced the concept of having multiple, custom layouts for different display sizes. This app supports a tablet mode! (as seen on the portfolio card)

Challenges

  • Fragment Containers
  • Recycler View
  • File read/write
  • Adapting for different device layouts
